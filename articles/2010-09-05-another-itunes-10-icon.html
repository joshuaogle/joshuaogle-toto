title: Another iTunes 10 Icon date: 2010/09/05 category: portfolio logo: 20100905001.png

Along with a large number of designers on Dribbble and across the internet, I've built my own replacement icon for iTunes 10. I think Apple really missed the mark by going a different direction than their other icons and the mobile side of the iTunes branding, along with a general lack of polish to the icon.

I've been using it for a couple of days now, and I love it. Here it is if you want to give it a try too. Includes icons for Mac OSX and Windows, a PNG version and the original PSD for you to tweak if you want. Enjoy.
